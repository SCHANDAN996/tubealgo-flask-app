Data Calculation and Metrics

To provide you with insights, we calculate certain metrics based on the data we receive from third-party APIs. Here is an overview of our calculation methods:

  • Views per Day: This is calculated by dividing a video's total views by the number of days since its publication. Formula: Total Views / Days Since Upload.
  • Engagement Rate: This is an estimated percentage. Formula: ((Likes + Comments) / Views) * 100.
  • Comment Sentiment: We use a Natural Language Processing (NLP) library to analyze comment text and determine if it is positive, negative, or neutral.

These metrics are estimations designed to provide trends and insights, not guaranteed facts.
