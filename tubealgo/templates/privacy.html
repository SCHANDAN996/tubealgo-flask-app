This Privacy Policy describes how TubeAlgo ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our services. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

1. Use of YouTube API Services

TubeAlgo uses YouTube API Services to provide its features. By using our application, you are also agreeing to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service. We encourage you to review the Google Privacy Policy for information on Google's data practices.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information You Provide: When you register for an account, you provide us with personal information, such as your email address and password.
  • Information from YouTube API Services: When you connect your Google Account, we access data from your YouTube channel as authorized by you. This may include:
    • Public channel data (e.g., channel name, subscriber count, video statistics).
    • Authorized data for management features (e.g., permission to upload videos or update metadata on your behalf).
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use our Service, such as the features you use and the actions you take.

3. How We Use, Process, and Share Your Information

We use, process, and store your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service. For example, we use your channel data to display analytics on your dashboard and provide competitor insights.
  • Allow you to manage your YouTube content through features like our 'YT Manager' and 'Upload Video' tools.
  • Communicate with you, including sending you service-related announcements or responding to your support inquiries.
  • Ensure the security of our Service and prevent fraud.

All collected data is stored securely in our database. We do not sell or share your private or authorized data with any external third parties for marketing purposes. Internal access to this data is restricted to authorized personnel for troubleshooting and user support.

4. Cookies and Device Information

We may store, access, or collect information on your device by using cookies or similar technology. This is used for essential functionalities like maintaining your login session and remembering your preferences. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. We will also retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Data Revocation and Deletion

You can revoke TubeAlgo's access to your Google Account at any time via the Google security settings page. Revoking access will prevent our application from accessing any further data from your YouTube channel.

In addition to revoking access, you can request the permanent deletion of all data we have stored related to your account. To do so, please navigate to your Settings page within the TubeAlgo application and use the "Request Data Deletion" feature.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with Personal Information, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at tubealgoofficial@gmail.com.
